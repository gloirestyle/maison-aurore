The Beginning

Founded on a belief in permanence.

Maison Aurore was born from a simple conviction: that design should outlast its moment. In a world of accelerating consumption, we chose the opposite — to make fewer things, more carefully, with materials that improve over years of use. Our founding principle was restraint. Our ambition: to create objects that become indispensable.

Material Philosophy

The material dictates the form.

Every piece in our collection begins with a material — chosen before a silhouette is drawn. The leather, the silk, the cashmere comes first. It tells us what it wants to become. We listen.

Calfskin

From certified European tanneries. Vegetable-tanned, chrome-free, hand-finished with natural oils. Selected for grain, weight, and how it softens with use.

Silk

Heavy washed silk from mills in Como. 22 momme weight minimum. Bias-cut to flow with gravity — never forced into shape.

Cashmere

Mongolian cashmere, 15.5 micron fiber diameter. Spun in Scotland, knitted in Italy. One goat yields enough fiber for two sweaters per year.

Wool

Italian-milled from Biella. Super 150s merino blended with cashmere at 480g/m. Heavy enough to hold its shape, light enough to move freely.

The Journey

Milestones

2024

Founding

Maison Aurore is established in Paris with a manifesto: to make objects for the rituals you return to. The first collection — five leather goods — is produced in a single atelier.

2024

First Collection

The Aurore Soft Carryall launches. Hand-finished calfskin, brass hardware, no logos. Sold out in three weeks through word of mouth alone.

2025

Ready-to-Wear

The collection expands into ready-to-wear essentials — Italian wool overcoats, heavy silk dresses, Mongolian cashmere knits. Production remains small-batch.

2025

Eau de Maison

The first fragrance — №1 — is composed in Grasse. Amber, leather, fig leaf. Housed in a frosted glass bottle with a polished brass cap.

2026

Objects of Desire

The Spring/Summer campaign — shot in the south of France. The digital flagship opens. The collection now spans leather, ready-to-wear, objects, footwear, and fragrance.

The Atelier

Where patience becomes form.

Our workshop is a quiet place. North-facing windows. Worn oak workbenches. The tools of three generations of artisans. Here, every stitch is placed by hand, every edge is burnished, every surface is touched repeatedly until it reaches the standard we require. There is no shortcut to this kind of beauty.
